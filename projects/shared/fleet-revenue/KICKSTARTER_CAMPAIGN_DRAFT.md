## Story (paste into Project Description)

### Opening

Hi — I'm building **PixelSports**, a fleet of free browser games with procedural pixel art. No loot boxes. No paywall on the web versions. You can play everything today on [itch.io](https://subtiliorars.itch.io) for free and tip if you want.

This campaign funds **Season 1 polish**: better audio, Steam releases for two flagship titles, and a bundled "Office Quarter" expansion for *Driving Me Nuts*.

### What's playable RIGHT NOW (free)

- **PixelSports Hub** — volleyball, bocce, curling, golf, dorodango
- **Yes Man** & **No Is a Complete Sentence** — cozy idle comedy pair
- **TradeGame** — paper trading sim (education, not financial advice)
- **Driving Me Nuts** — peanut food-truck RPG slice
- **Fleet demos** — Sortie dogfight, Broadside naval ops, and more

### What you're funding

| Deliverable | Backer gets |
|-------------|-------------|
| Season 1 audio pass | SFX + music stubs → shipped tracks in all hub games |
| Steam release: Beach Volleyball | Steam key (tier $8+) |
| Steam release: Driving Me Nuts EA | Steam key (tier $12+) |
| Office Quarter expansion | New district + 10 events (all $5+ backers) |
| Name in credits | $15+ tier |
| Design a peanut pun NPC | $50 limited tier (10 slots) |

### Why Kickstarter vs just tips?

Tips on itch/Ko-fi keep the lights on. Kickstarter lets us **pre-sell Steam boxes** and commission audio at scale without gambling rent money.

### Risks

- Solo dev + AI-assisted pipeline — schedule slips by 4–8 weeks possible
- Steam review timing not fully controlled
- Scope is Season 1 polish, not a AAA MMO

### Transparency

Code is built in public repos. itch builds update weekly when possible. Backers get a private Discord dev channel.
