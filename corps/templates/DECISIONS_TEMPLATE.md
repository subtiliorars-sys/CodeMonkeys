# DECISIONS — __REPO__

> Append-only log of significant choices, so a new agent or human never re-litigates a
> settled call. Recommended by `agent-corps/agent-governance.md` (§ Per-repo DECISIONS.md).
> Keep entries terse. One entry per significant choice. Newest at the top.

Each entry records: **what was chosen**, **what was rejected**, **why**, and **what risk
was accepted**. That last field is the one people skip and later regret — fill it.
